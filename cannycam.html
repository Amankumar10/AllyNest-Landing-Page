About CannyCam

CannyCam transforms traditional cameras into intelligent monitoring systems capable of detecting behavior, emotional signals, and potential safety risks in real time.

Instead of simply recording events, CannyCam analyzes video streams and provides meaningful alerts that help people respond faster and make safer decisions.

Key Capabilities

  • AI behavior and motion detection
  • Emotion and stress detection
  • Real-time alerts and insights
  • Works with existing CCTV infrastructure
  • Scalable for homes, businesses, and smart cities
