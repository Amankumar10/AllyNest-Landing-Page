About Compatifi

Compatifi is an AI-powered compatibility and social connection platform designed to help people build emotionally aligned relationships across friendships, romance, workplace connections, and personal growth.

Unlike traditional dating or social platforms that focus on quick matches, Compatifi focuses on emotional understanding, communication quality, and long-term relationship growth.

Key Features

  • AI-driven emotional compatibility insights
  • Guided conversations for deeper understanding
  • Relationship growth tools and goals
  • Emotion-aware social interaction
  • AI suggestions to improve communication
